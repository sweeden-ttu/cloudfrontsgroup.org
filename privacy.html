Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect only information you voluntarily provide through our contact form and newsletter signup (name, email address, message content). We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services that collect personal data.

How We Use Your Information

To respond to your inquiries, send you requested updates (if subscribed), and improve our services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
