Our Services

Technology access, data analytics, and program delivery for Central Texas communities

📊

HUD Data Analytics

Analysis of HUD NOFO datasets, housing market trends, demographic indicators, and program effectiveness metrics for public-sector clients. Includes housing inventory and price trend analysis, emerging market identification, tax abatement scenario modeling, city budget and demographic analysis, foreclosure market intelligence.

Learn More
🏛

Section 3 Compliance

Comprehensive Section 3 compliance support for housing authorities, including strategic planning, benchmarking, reporting, and technical assistance. We help PHAs meet HUD regulatory requirements and maximize economic opportunities for low-income residents.

Learn More
🌿

USDA Farming Program Navigation

Guidance on USDA farm loans (FSA direct/guaranteed), conservation programs (EQIP, CSP, CRP), rural development grants, and beginning farmer/rancher resources. We help farmers and rural communities navigate federal agriculture programs.

Learn More
⚙

AI/ML Research

Theoretical and applied AI safety research, including our AAAI 2026 publication on bounded-agent security guardrails for government intelligence reporting. Research partnerships with academic collaborators.

Learn More
✍

Grant Writing & Federal Funding

Support for identifying, applying to, and managing federal grant programs including HUD NOFOs, USDA rural development grants, CDBG, and other federal assistance programs.

Learn More
🚀

Community Technology Access

Community technology access programs, innovation delivery services, and civic technology tools that make advanced capabilities available to underserved communities in Central Texas.

Learn More

All services are available on a fee-for-service, grant-funded, or pro-bono basis depending on community need and available resources.
