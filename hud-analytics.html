Methodology & Approach

1 Data Sourcing & Provenance

We source data from HUD, Census Bureau, Bureau of Labor Statistics, local government open data portals, and proprietary market datasets. Every data point is tagged with source, timestamp, and collection methodology for full auditability.

2 Guardrail-Verified Analysis

All analytical outputs are processed through our bounded-agent security framework, ensuring formal verification of inference chains, anomaly detection, and adversarial input screening before results are presented.

3 Human Review & Validation

Every analytics product is reviewed by domain experts who validate findings against ground-truth data and local knowledge before delivery to clients.
