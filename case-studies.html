Case Studies

Real projects, measurable impact in Central Texas communities

Section 3 Strategic Plan Development for Central Texas PHA

Location: Central Texas

Analysis of HUD regulatory requirements and PHA administrative data. Developed benchmarking framework, strategic goals, and reporting templates.

Result: PHA achieved first-ever compliant Section 3 Annual Report with measurable beneficiary outcomes.

HUD NOFO Dataset Analysis for Regional Housing Assessment

Location: Central Texas

Compiled and analyzed HUD NOFO datasets across four Central Texas counties. Identified emerging housing market trends, demographic shifts, and program coverage gaps.

Result: Data-driven recommendations adopted by local housing authority for FY 2027 funding priorities.

USDA Farm Loan Navigation for Beginning Farmer

Location: Central Texas

Guided beginning farmer through FSA direct farm ownership loan application. Prepared business plan, financial projections, and eligibility documentation.

Result: Client received loan approval for 40-acre operation; now enrolled in Conservation Stewardship Program.

Community Technology Access Program Pilot

Location: Central Texas

Deployed civic technology tools and digital literacy resources in underserved neighborhood. Partnered with local community organizations for outreach and training.

Result: 150+ residents gained access to online services, 85% reported increased digital confidence.
