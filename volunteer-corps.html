Community Service

Cloud Fronts Volunteer Corps

Building technology access, data literacy, and civic engagement through community-powered service initiatives across Central Texas.

The Volunteer Corps is the hands-on service arm of Cloud Fronts Group. We recruit, train, and deploy volunteers to support technology access programs, data literacy initiatives, and community engagement projects — with a focus on underserved communities and Section 3 worker populations.

Program Areas

💻

Technology Access

Refurbishing and distributing hardware, setting up community WiFi, providing basic tech support and digital literacy training for low-income residents.

📈

Data Literacy

Teaching community members how to access and understand public data — housing market information, demographic trends, and government program data.

📞

Civic Engagement

Voter registration drives, candidate forum support, judicial election education, and community meeting facilitation.

🏛

Section 3 Pathways

Mentoring and skill-building for Section 3 workers, connecting volunteers with training programs, apprenticeships, and contracting opportunities.

Current Initiatives

Active

Community Data Access Project

Volunteers help community organizations access and interpret HUD data, Census Bureau statistics, and local housing market indicators. Training and materials provided.

Active

Digital Skills Workshop Series

Free workshops covering computer basics, job application tools, online safety, and accessing government services online. Targeted at Section 3 worker communities.

Seasonal

Judicial Election Education

Nonpartisan voter education on judicial candidates, court operations, and the importance of transparent judicial elections. Materials and candidate profiles available.

Get Involved

Volunteers needed. We welcome community members, students, tech professionals, and retirees. No prior experience required — training is provided. Current focus areas: Central Texas (Bell County, Williamson County, Travis County).
