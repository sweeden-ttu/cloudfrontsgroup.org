Current Research Priorities

1. HUD Demonstrations

Gleaning knowledge from outcomes of HUD pilot and demonstration programs.

2. Housing as a Platform

Understanding how housing improves quality of life (health, education, employment).

3. American Housing Survey

Increasing analytical use of AHS microdata.

4. Housing Technology

Innovations in housing construction methods, materials, and systems.

How to Submit

  1. Develop a research concept aligned with one of the four priority areas above.
  2. Identify cost-share partners (foundations, state/local agencies, other federal programs) to cover 50% of costs.
  3. Email your proposal to ResearchPartnerships@hud.gov
