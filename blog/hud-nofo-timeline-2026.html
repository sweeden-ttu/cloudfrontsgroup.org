HUD NOFO Timeline 2026 — Key Deadlines for Housing Research Grants

Each year, HUD's Office of Policy Development and Research (PD&R) publishes a Notice of Funding Opportunity (NOFO) for housing research grants. For organizations tracking these opportunities, here are the key milestones in the 2026 cycle.

Note: This timeline is based on historical patterns. Confirm all dates on Grants.gov.

Two-Phase Review Process

Most HUD research NOFOs follow a two-phase review:

  • Phase 1 (Concept Paper): A short narrative outlining the research question, methodology, and budget estimate. Typically due in late summer.
  • Phase 2 (Full Application): Invited only. Complete proposal with detailed budget, work plan, team bios, and supporting documentation.

Typical Timeline

MilestoneApproximate Date
NOFO published in Federal RegisterJune – July 2026
Phase 1 concept papers due30 – 45 days after publication
Phase 2 invitations issued60 days after Phase 1 deadline
Full applications due60 – 90 days after invitation
Award announcementsJanuary – March 2027
Project start datesApril – June 2027

Preparing Now

Organizations can prepare well before the NOFO drops:

  • Register or renew SAM.gov registration (must be current at time of application)
  • Identify and confirm research team members
  • Draft a conceptual framework and methodology outline
  • Establish indirect cost rate agreement if applicable
  • Gather letters of support from community partners
