ONGOING  Federal Contracting — SAM.gov Registration

HUD's PD&R is "looking for small businesses with the capacity to conduct research and evaluation related to HUD programs." Register on SAM.gov to bid on HUD contracts.

Registration Steps

  1. Get your Unique Entity Identifier (UEI)
    Go to SAM.gov →
    Free. Click "Get Started" under Entity Registration.
  2. Register your entity in SAM.gov
    Provide: legal name, address, EIN/Tax ID, banking info, NAICS codes, points of contact.
    Registration is FREE — do not pay third parties. Renew annually.
  3. Complete Representations & Certifications
    Small business size standards, NAICS self-certification, etc.
  4. Browse HUD contracting opportunities
    SAM.gov Contract Opportunities · HUD Forecast of Contracting Opportunities
  5. Submit proposals/bids per solicitation instructions
